About Book My Wing Icon
Nestled in the prime locale of Thane, Risland The Icon is a luxurious residential complex that will offer residents everything they have ever desired in their dream home. Hailing from the well-respected Risland Group, this project is currently under development and is scheduled for possession in December 2025.
Built over a large parcel of land of 6.8 acres, The Icon features a cutting-edge and avant-garde S.H.I.E.L.D security system that helps guard your homes all the time. The upscale and posh residential complex offers 2 & 3 urban-chic homes that are sure to complement your lifestyle. You can enjoy the array of amenities offered such as swimming pools, badminton court, jogging track, kids' play area, Icon club, and many more.

About the Locality: Commuting is extremely hassle-free as Risland The Icon is well-connected to the Eastern Express Highway, Ghodbunder Road, Thane railway station, and the upcoming metro station. Educational Institutions such as DAV Public School and C.P. Goenka International School as well as Bethany Hospital and Jupiter Hospital are in close proximity. Get your shopping fix with Lake City Mall and others. You can go on a day trip to the Tikuji-ni-Wadi water park.
